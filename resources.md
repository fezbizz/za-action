## Civic & Community Structures

- **Community Policing Forums (CPFs)** — exist at every SAPS precinct. Attend monthly meetings.
- **Ward Committees** — local governance. Your ward councillor holds public meetings.
- **National Youth Development Agency (NYDA)** — youth empowerment and participation.

## Key Legislation (read online)

- **Immigration Act 13 of 2002** (as amended) — legal framework for entry, stay, and deportation
- **Refugees Act 130 of 1998** — refugee status and asylum procedures
- **South African Citizenship Act 88 of 1995** — citizenship requirements
- **Constitution of SA, Section 33** — Just administration (right to fair administrative action)

## How to Verify Information

Before sharing anything about immigration:
1. Check source — is it a reputable news outlet?
2. Cross-reference with official government statements
3. Look for the same story from multiple independent sources
4. Beware of content that dehumanizes or incites violence
5. Report harmful misinformation to the Real411 platform
